Types of Website

When discussing new creations with our clients, we have in depth discussions to formulate exactly the kind of website our clients need. The following are some of the broad categories.

  • Business Card - A number of pages which explain your business and your services / products, list your opening hours and display your location and contact information.
  • E-Commerce Website - If you want enable your customers to buy your products / services online, you will need an interactive site, where users can perform actions such as 'adding items to their shopping cart', 'proceed to payment' etc.
  • Dynamic Content Website - Should you need a website which requires that you update the content frequently, we can build a site which has a simple and user friendly interface allowing non technical personnel to easily update the articles / pages which appear on the website. This site is built and delivered with a Content Management System, for example, Joomla or Wordpress. Full training is provided on how to use and update the site.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

All our creations are built to be optimised for search engines. This means that your site will be built in a way which encourages Google and other search engines to place your site high on the list of search results. We do not promise that you will be number one for all search results and you should be wary of anybody that does. We do promise to optimise your site according to the latest industry standards.
